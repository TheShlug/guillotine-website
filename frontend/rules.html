🪓 Guillotine League Rules 🪓

Survive or be eliminated. Only one team remains.

🏈 How It Works

The Guillotine League begins with 18 teams. Each week, league results are determined by the total points from each team's starting lineup. Head-to-head matchups do not matter.

After each week, the team that scored the fewest points that week is eliminated from the competition. When a team is eliminated, its entire roster of players become free agents, eligible to be acquired by the rest of the league.

One team is eliminated each week from weeks 1 - 17 of the NFL season until one team remains as the champion.

📝 Draft

  • Format: Snake draft
  • Rounds: 12 rounds
  • Pick clock: 90 seconds to start (may decrease in later rounds)

💰 Waivers & Free Agency

  • FAAB Budget: $1,000 per team
  • Waiver processing: Wednesdays at 8:00 PM EST
  • Minimum bid: $0
  • After waivers clear: Remaining free agents available on first-come-first-serve (FCFS) basis
  • Preseason waivers: One blind bidding period on the Wednesday before the NFL season opener

⚠ Important Week 14+ Rule: Players on teams that are chopped from weeks 14 onward will NOT be available in free agency. The last actual "chop" releasing players will be from the team eliminated in week 13. Waivers with available players will continue to run through the end of the season (week 17).

🚫 Trading

Trading players is NOT allowed.

All roster building must be done through the draft and waiver wire. This ensures fair competition and prevents collusion.
